About the artist

Hi! I'm Ash, and I run Bluesilverarts. I'm a freelance digital artist who has been doing art professionally since 2018, starting from creating portraits at markets for $10. Since then my business has expanded to include an esty shop, weekend children's art classes and contracted work. I consider my greatest inspirations to be Hieu (Kelogsloops), Laura Brouwers (Cyarine) and Tami Muedinover (Tamaytka).

I am currently an arts stream student at Northern College of the Arts and Technology

Bluesilverarts is based in Greater Melbourne, Victoria
